Steve Jobs

Visionary Co‑Founder of Apple and Tech Innovator

About Steve

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur and inventor who co‑founded Apple Inc. He transformed industries including personal computing, animated movies, music, phones, tablet computing, and digital publishing.

Timeline of Key Events

  • 1955: Born in San Francisco, California.
  • 1976: Co‑founded Apple Computer with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne.
  • 1984: Launched the Macintosh, revolutionizing graphical user interfaces.
  • 1985: Left Apple and founded NeXT, Inc.
  • 1997: Returned to Apple, becoming CEO and leading the company’s resurgence.
  • 2001: Introduced the iPod, changing the music industry.
  • 2007: Launched the iPhone, redefining smartphones.
  • 2010: Unveiled the iPad, popularizing tablet computing.
  • 2011: Passed away, leaving an enduring legacy in technology and design.
